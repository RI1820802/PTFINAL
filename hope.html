Message of Hope

That feeling of being in the dark, its all too familiar isn't it?
It's as if you've been there.
After all, grief and despair is a part of everyones humanity.
Though this is true doesn't mean we have to stay in the dark though.
Despair may be a part of us but so is hope.
Hope is not attained but something unlocked.
It's something you must uncover deep in your heart.
Which is why there's no such thing as a "hopeless'' situation.
So as long as you have your heart intact of course.

The design we've created simply embodies
that after a depressing night is a beautiful morning.
Or in simpler terms, giving everyone hope that
after all the trouble and pain we've been
through is a better life for all of us.
That the sunrise will always come.

BE HOPEFUL!
